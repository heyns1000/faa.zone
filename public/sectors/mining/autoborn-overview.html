What exactly is AutoBorn™?

AutoBorn™ isn’t another dispatch program. It’s a full AI ecosystem that thinks, reacts, and scales on your behalf. Imagine every truck, shovel and sensor in your pit acting like a single, coordinated swarm.

  • Central Brain → Auto-orchestrates equipment, crews, and haulage in real time.
  • PulseGrid™ Sync → 9-second updates keep every machine on the same plan.
  • VaultChain™ Security → Tamper-proof audit trail of every load and decision.
  • ClauseIndex™ Compliance → One-click legal and ESG reporting.

Central Brain

A cloud AI that never sleeps. Pulls live data from every vehicle, replans haul routes, and pushes new tasks back out — in seconds.

  • Live fleet map & queue prediction
  • Adaptive speed & route limits
  • Blend optimization to hit plant grade

PulseGrid™ (9-second heartbeat)

Every truck, drill, and drone sends a packet every nine seconds. All packets align to one clock, so the AI sees a synchronized snapshot.

  • GNSS position, payload, tire pressure
  • Engine health & fuel burn
  • Safety events (hard brake, overspeed)

VaultChain™ Security

An append-only ledger: new blocks are written, old ones can’t be changed. Any tampering breaks the hash chain and is rejected.

  • SHA-256 Merkle roots per shift
  • On-prem signing + cloud replication
  • Auditor read-only portals

ClauseIndex™ Compliance

A rule engine that converts VaultChain™ data into PDF reports and ESG metrics.

  • Safety, Environmental & HR clauses
  • Immediate breach alerts
  • Auto-generated compliance packs
