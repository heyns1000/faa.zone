Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deployment take?

A pilot VaultMesh node is usually live in ≈ 14 days. Full fleet roll-out takes around 4–12 weeks, depending on scale.

Will it replace my existing dispatch system?

AutoBorn™ can integrate alongside your current dispatch or replace it entirely. Many sites start in overlay mode, then switch to full AutoBorn™ control.

Is my data really immutable?

Yes. Every transaction is written to FAA VaultChain™—an append-only, cryptographically linked ledger that cannot be altered without consensus.
